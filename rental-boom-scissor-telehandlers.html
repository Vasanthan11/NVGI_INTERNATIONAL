Boom lifts, scissor lifts and telehandlers – aligned to working height and reach

We focus on matching the right access equipment to your task – façade works, MEP installation, maintenance shutdowns or warehouse operations. Discuss your application and we help you choose a suitable platform.

Boom Lifts

Articulated and telescopic boom lifts for outdoor and indoor applications requiring outreach.

  • Working heights suitable for façade & high-level works.
  • Options for rough-terrain and slab applications.
  • Diesel and electric configurations (based on availability).

Scissor Lifts

Platform access where vertical height and platform space are more important than outreach.

  • Indoor electric scissors for warehouses and malls.
  • Rough-terrain scissors for construction sites.
  • Suitable for MEP, fit-out and maintenance tasks.

Telehandlers

Material handling equipment combining lifting height with forward reach for site logistics.

  • Suitable for pallet and bulk handling.
  • Useful for multi-level construction projects.
  • Options for different capacities (subject to availability).

Safety & support

Focus on safe use, basic checks and responsive support

Access equipment is most effective when used correctly. We encourage trained operators, adherence to safety practices and basic daily checks by users.

  • Basic familiarisation at the time of delivery (where applicable).
  • Encouragement of pre-use checks by operators.
  • Guidance on ground conditions and safe positioning.
  • Support for reporting issues during the rental period.
  • Options to discuss machines for specific confined or complex areas.

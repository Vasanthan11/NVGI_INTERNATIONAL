Why used machinery

Balance between budget, working height and project duration

Used boom lifts, scissor lifts and telehandlers can be a practical choice when you need equipment for multiple projects over time, but want to stay within defined budgets. We focus on clarity around condition and suitability rather than just age or hours.

  • Helps reduce upfront capital compared to new equipment.
  • Useful when you frequently rent equipment of similar height.
  • Can support multi-year project pipelines where utilisation is expected.
  • Options to mix used purchase with occasional rentals for peak periods.
